How it works

The Pimp My Cause web platform is free for both marketers and causes and is made available by the social enterprise MarketingKind.

Step one

Fill in the registration form, then check your email and click on the link in the Pimp My Cause account activation email.

Step two

Complete your profile as a cause or as a marketer. You can always view, edit or delete your profile by clicking on your name in the top right corner of the screen (after signing in). Make sure to upload your photo or cause logo to attract more members to your profile.

Step three

If you are a cause make sure to create at least one help wanted ad so that our marketing members are aware of what marketing assistance you need. You can always add, view, delete or edit your help wanted ads by logging in, clicking on your name in the top right corner of your screen button (after signing in).

Step four

Search and browse on the Find a Marketer and Find a Cause pages to find the volunteer marketer(s) or cause(s) you would like to work with. Send messages expressing your interest and your goals. 

Step five

Make sure to let us know about the work that gets done through Pimp My Cause by emailing anna@marketingkind.org.
