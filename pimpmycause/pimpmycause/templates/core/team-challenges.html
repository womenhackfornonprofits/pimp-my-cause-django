Team Challenges

Is your CFO asking you to spend less?

And is your CEO asking you to accomplish more?

Challenges are dedicated to building marketing teams that accomplish more with less. We achieve this by fostering a team’s purposefulness, creativity and collaboration.

What is a challenge?

Challenges are talent development programmes with a difference.

They provide live charity briefs that have to be fulfilled with zero or low budgets and that are built around a team’s talent development needs and with expert mentors and coaches available to support the process. The experience is transformational and results is a team that is able to deliver more efficiently and effectively on business priorities and the legacy of a positive social impact.
